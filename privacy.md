# Privacy Policy for iGPlus

**Effective Date:** May 03, 2026

iGPlus is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience on igpmanager.com. We value your privacy and this policy outlines how we handle data.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
iGPlus is a "local-first" extension. 
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any external servers. 
- **Local Processing:** All data processing, such as race strategy calculations or report formatting, happens entirely within your browser.

### 2. Google Drive Integration (Sync Feature)
If you choose to enable the Google Drive Cloud Sync feature:
- **Authentication:** The extension uses Google OAuth2 to authenticate. We never see your Google password.
- **Data Storage:** The extension only reads and writes specific configuration files (`config.json`, `strategies.json`, `reports.json`) that it creates within a folder named "iGPlus" on your personal Google Drive.
- **Spreadsheet Access:** If you use the spreadsheet export/import feature, the extension only accesses the specific spreadsheets you select.
- **User Control:** This data is stored on your personal Google Drive. We (the developers) have no access to your Google Drive files or the data you sync.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your data stays between your browser and your Google Drive.

### 4. Security
iGPlus uses standard browser security APIs and official Google OAuth libraries to ensure your data remains secure. 

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

### 6. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/R0b0To/iGPlus/issues).
